About OLSC Denver

Formed in January 2011 as the Colorado Reds, OLSC Denver became an Official Liverpool Supporters Club in 2013. Our goal is to provide local and visiting Liverpool FC fans an enjoyable and welcoming environment to come support our favorite football club.

The Abbey Tavern

OLSC Denver's home pub since 2014, the Abbey Tavern is owned and run by Liverpool supporters and is open for every televised Liverpool match.
